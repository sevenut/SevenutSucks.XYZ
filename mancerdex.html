Get familiar with the creatures of Dissonia!

Lesser Arboran

Dryad Nymph

Growth

Despite their cute appearance, lesser arborans hold a dispassionate view of life. They constantly seek out new knowledge, but derive no enjoyment from doing so.

Arboran

Dryad Nymph

Growth

Growth in an arboran is associated with an increase in magical power, rather than chronological age. Most arborans remain in this stage of life for their entire existence.

Elder Arboran

Dryad Nymph

Growth

It is unknown what causes an arboran to elevate to elder status. However, it is known that elder arborans treat life as precious, which is much unlike earlier stages in an arboran's life cycle.

Lesser Pisceran

Nereid Nymph

Flow

Lesser piscerans live in the ocean, drifting from one place to the next, with no particular goal in mind.

Pisceran

Nereid Nymph

Flow

With legs, piscerans can walk on land as well as traverse water. Piscerans constantly condense excess mana into pearl-like concretions. These concretions are highly sought after for both their aesthetic and practical value.

Elder Pisceran

Nereid Nymph

Flow

Elder piscerans are known for their incredible spellcrafting abilities. Many spells used to this day originate from elder piscerans.

Lesser Gaean

Oread Nymph

Heat

Lesser gaeans are trained vigorously by their superiors. As a result, lesser gaeans, and by extention all gaeans, are incredibly physically strong.

Gaean

Oread Nymph

Heat

Gaeans live in highly organized societies that value conformity above all else. Gaeans that step out of line are often shunned by other gaeans.

Elder Gaean

Oread Nymph

Heat

Elder gaeans do not live amongst gaean society. Rather, they roam the world alone, free from the conformity of gaean society.

Lesser Opon

Maenad Nymph

Dread

Lesser opons are highly sought after creatures for their ability to produce a variety of liquids. The greatest population of opons live in Purinam, which is a hotspot for opon trafficking.

Opon

Maenad Nymph

Dread

Opons are weak compared to other nymphs, and even humans. They are not particularly adept at spellcasting. Opons are never seen without a smile, but this does not indicate their emotional state.

Elder Opon

Maenad Nymph

Dread

It is said that every elder opon has killed someone, although the validity of this claim is disputed. There are no elder opons who live in captivity.

Lesser Wind Maiden

Aurae Nymph

Order

Lesser wind maidens are born in the skies. Little is known about lesser wind maidens, although it is known that they hold great magical power, even at a young age. They are drawn to power.

Wind Maiden

Aurae Nymph

Order

Wind maidens are mysterious creatures that rarely descend to the land below. They seem to monitor the events below them from the skies, but they never choose to intervene.

Elder Wind Maiden

Aurae Nymph

Order

Elder wind maidens are known for helping those in need. They never stick around, however. It is unknown why they do this, especially considering that younger wind maidens do not do this.

Monkoko

Plant

Growth

Monkokos are plants that imitate monkeys. They exhibit behavior much like a young monkey. This likely functions as a means to reduce predation.

Monosquatch

Plant

Growth

Monosquatches are tough enough to scare off common predators. Monosquatches eventually leave their troop to form their own troop.

Bigroot

Plant

Growth/Heat

One bigroot typically acts as the guardian for a troop of monosquatch and monkokos. Seeds grow from their heads and eventually drop off, becoming new monkokos.

Cniigrub

Insect

Flow

Cniigrubs have a limited amount of psychic ability. They deter predators by making them feel mentally odd.

Sleeping Idol

Insect

Flow

A sleeper is a cocoon with a tough hide. As it gets closer to hatching, it releases bouts of psychic disturbance. It is not safe to be around one as it matures.

Evil Eye

Insect

Flow

Evil eyes constantly emit a psychic signal that can invade the minds of all but the most powerful mages.

Groveman

Plant Elemental

Growth

Grovemen roam the jungle of the Turtle Isles, leaving fertile ground where they step. They are an invaluable part of the Isles' ecosystem. Many small animals take up home in the leaves of grovemen.

Orbid

Aberration

Null

These swamp creatures can consume nearly any substance with no ill effects. They are a largely sessile creature, not moving from their birth spot much, if at all.

Invasia

Plant Parasite

Growth/Dread

This plant parasitizes magical plant creatures. Just a single seed is enough to overtake any plant creature's body.

Tentacloid

Celestial Mollusk

Dread

Not much is known about tentacloids, but they are often found in grain fields. Strange patterns are found in the fields where tentacloids are sighted.

Cephalopoid

Celestial Mollusk

Dread

Livestock is known to go missing whenever cephalopoids are spotted.

Ironshell

Mollusk

Heat

Ironshells are named for the high iron content in their shells. They are often harvested due to this fact, which kills the ironshell.

Scaleshell

Mollusk

Heat

Scaleshell scales can be used to create strong scale mail armor. They are made of a metal tougher than steel.

Spiddle

Spider

Growth

This cute creature is a popular pet. They are deeply loyal and loving.

Creepider

Spider

Growth

The natural predator of spiddles. Creepiders are often found near where pet spiddles go missing.

Strangerbread

Golem

Order

Strangerbreads are golems of unknown creator. They are often found raiding food storages for cookie ingredients, which they use to make more strangerbreads.

Pitter

Spirit

Flow

A vengeful spirit of sorts. Their presence indicates an imminent storm.

Patter

Spirit

Flow/Dread

Any evidence regarding the existence of patters is apocryphal at best.

Common Morpha

Shapeshifter

Cat-like: Growth, Owl-like: Flow, Amphibian-like: Heat

As the easiest creature to contract, novice spellcasters often have the common morpha as their first familiar. They are shapeshifters that take the form that most suits their master's need.

Alloy

Construct

Order

A construct of a bygone age. The alloy's original use is unknown. However, they have been repurposed in the current age in Lutum as guards.

Alumiknight

Construct

Heat/Order

Alumiknights are given to Lutum government officials to serve as bodyguards.

Dupanthrope

Elemental Aberration

Order

A very peculiar creature. Dupanthropes are seemingly made of human flesh. They always appear facing the observer, no matter where the observer is, or how many observers there are. Their cry instills a sense of dread into all humans that hear it, although curiously this does not affect non-humans.

Deadarm

Zombie

Dread

The deadarm is a necromancer's mockery of death.

Deadlift

Zombie

Dread

Deadmeats are not more than a few deadarms sewn together.

Deadweight

Zombie

Dread

Deadweights are again, more deadmeats and deadarms stitched together. This hulking monstrosity is any necromancer's muscle.

Laughing Flame

Elemental Djinn

Heat

Laughing flames are mischevious creatures that like to burn things for amusement, although they always ensure not to bring bodily harm to any living being.

Librederm

Zombie Construct

Dread

It is said that the pages of a librederm are full of mad ramblings of a long dead wizard. However, this is apocryphal at best as these notes are written in a script nobody can read.

Sand Grub

Dragon

Heat

The bite strength of a sand grub is enough to rip off a bugbear's arm clean off.

Sand Serpent

Dragon

Heat

Sand serpents are highly territorial. They will fight to the death over territory disputes.

Desert Terror

Dragon

Heat

If you feel the ground shake, that is a desert terror travelling. If you feel the ground shake directly below you, that is a desert terror finding a morsel to eat.

Strato Angler

Elemental Fish

Flow

These elementals bear a superficial resemblance to anglerfish, hence the name. The strato angler is often seen flying high above the ocean.

Everythling

Changeling

Null

The apex predator of the swamp. Eveythlings can change the shape of their bodies to suit their needs and can perfectly imitate sounds they hear.

Apotherald

Celestial

Order

An angel that one day descended from heaven above.
